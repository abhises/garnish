Dive into the heart of Hollywood with Garnish Music Production School and International American University (IAU). Our Undergraduate Business and Music programs fuses elite music production training with top tier business education, offering ASBA and BBA degrees for international students. Aspiring artists, producers, and audio engineers learn from music industry legends and earn a degree to dominate the global music business!

 

 

Who is this course for?

  • Artists, Songwriters, Producers & DJs ready to kick start their music journey
  • International students aiming for an ASBA or BBA degree in the United States with a focus on music education
  • Professionals aiming to patch up any holes in their knowledge, network with LA's best and acquire business skills for the music industry

• Perfect for: Artists and Producers hungry for skills to kickstart their music and business game
• Outcome: ASBA Degree + Garnish Certificate with tracks ready to drop in your portfolio
